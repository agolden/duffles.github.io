February 4: code snippets based on Alec's /people card

While thinking through the effect for Alec's card, I broke down my building process into a few steps. First, I had to remove Alec's normal headshot image and replace it with my head-on-a-bike image. Second, I had to move that new image of the screen, so it would appear to be biking away. Third, I needed to return to the original image in its original location.

Below, you can see what we call "code snippets" inspired by this larger process. By breaking a more complicated program into small pieces, it can be easier to figure out what needs to happen in order to get your desired effect.

January 28: people card mock-ups for Alec

Final codepen with card effect can be found here.

But this is just one of a few ideas going in. Here are the sketches I made as I brainstormed and talked about the card with Alec.

1. Look at me / Don't look at me

Alec is a shy and quiet person. He doesn't like to make loud noises, to be involved in physical comedy, to speak in public. So, I thought he'd love having his card sporlighted whenever people mouse over it!

2. Does a head on a bicycle have consciousness?

Alec bicycles everywhere. Including off the /people page, to avoid your attention.

3. Robot on hover

Alec is something of an extreme person. He keeps a strict, structured, and very full schedule. He can forego sleep and food. He believes in optimal solutions. Is he secretly a robot?
